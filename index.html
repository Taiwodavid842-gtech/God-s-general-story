God's General Story
Derek Prince
The Bible Teacher Who Dedicated His Life to Explaining God's Word
Derek Prince was one of the twentieth century's most respected Bible teachers. He became known for his clear, systematic teaching on Scripture, covering subjects such as faith, prayer, spiritual warfare, blessing and cursing, the Holy Spirit, and the authority of God's Word. Through books, radio broadcasts, and sermons, his ministry has reached millions around the world. Early Life Derek Prince was born on August 14, 1915, in Bangalore, where his British parents were living at the time. He was educated in England and proved to be an outstanding student. He attended Eton College and later studied at King's College, Cambridge, specializing in Greek, Latin, philosophy, and ancient languages. At that time, he considered himself a philosopher rather than a Christian. His Conversion During World War II, Derek served in the British Army as a medical orderly. While in the army, he began reading the Bible, especially the book of Romans. As he read, he had a life-changing encounter with Jesus Christ. He later said that he made two resolutions: "First, I would never again pass a day without reading my Bible. Second, I would never again read the Bible merely as a philosophical book, but as the Word of God speaking to me." Those commitments shaped the rest of his life. Called to Ministry After the war, Derek felt called to teach the Bible. He became known for explaining difficult biblical subjects in a simple and practical way. His teaching emphasized that Christians should build every belief and practice on Scripture. Marriage and Family While serving in the Middle East, Derek met Lydia Prince, a Danish missionary who cared for orphaned Jewish girls. They married and together raised a large family, including several adopted daughters. After Lydia's death in 1975, Derek later married Ruth Prince, who also shared his passion for ministry. Worldwide Ministry Derek Prince traveled extensively, teaching the Bible in churches, conferences, and seminars across many nations. He founded what is now known as Derek Prince Ministries, which continues translating and distributing his teachings in numerous languages. His radio programs, books, and recorded messages have helped believers grow in their understanding of Scripture. Major Teachings Derek Prince became especially known for teaching on: The authority of the Bible. Faith and obedience. Prayer and fasting. The Holy Spirit. Spiritual warfare. The power of blessing. The cross of Christ. God's covenant with His people. Christian discipleship. One of his best-known books is: The Exchange at the Cross In it, he explains how Jesus took humanity's sin, curse, and punishment so believers could receive forgiveness, righteousness, and God's blessing. Challenges Derek Prince's life was not without difficulties. 1. World War II Serving during the war exposed him to hardship and uncertainty. 2. Personal Loss The death of his first wife, Lydia, was a painful experience. 3. Opposition Some of his teachings, particularly on spiritual warfare and deliverance, generated debate among Christians. Despite differing views, Derek consistently urged people to examine his teaching by the Scriptures. 4. Constant Travel Years of international ministry required great sacrifice and long periods away from home. His Successes By God's grace, Derek Prince: Authored more than 50 books. Produced hundreds of Bible teaching messages. Helped establish Bible teaching ministries in many nations. Reached millions through radio, television, and printed materials. Encouraged Christians worldwide to study and obey God's Word. His teachings continue to be translated into over 100 languages. His Final Years Even in his later years, Derek remained committed to teaching the Bible. He continued writing and recording messages until shortly before his death. On September 24, 2003, Derek Prince went home to be with the Lord at the age of 88. Lessons from Derek Prince's Life 1. Build Your Life on God's Word The Bible should be the final authority for faith and practice. 2. Never Stop Learning Derek remained a dedicated student of Scripture throughout his life. 3. Obey God's Voice He believed that God's blessings follow obedience. 4. Persevere Through Trials Difficulties became opportunities to trust God more deeply. 5. Teach Others Knowledge of God's Word should be shared to strengthen the Church. Conclusion Derek Prince's life is a testimony to the transforming power of God's Word. From a brilliant philosopher and scholar to a devoted Bible teacher, he spent more than fifty years helping believers understand and apply Scripture. His legacy continues through his books, sermons, and global ministry, encouraging Christians everywhere to know the Bible, obey God, and live by faith.
